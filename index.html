Welcome to Dr. Mower

Your trusted partner in lawn equipment repair and maintenance since 1995. With over 25 years of experience, we provide expert repair services for all types of lawn equipment, from mowers to trimmers and everything in between.

About Us

Dr. Mower was founded by John Smith, a certified small engine repair specialist with a passion for quality workmanship. Our team of experienced technicians is committed to providing fast, reliable service at competitive prices.

We pride ourselves on:

  • Certified technicians

  • Warranty on all repairs
  • Genuine replacement parts
